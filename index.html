What is it?

Information technology it is a system of methods, processes and ways of using computing equipment and communication systems for creating, collecting, transmitting, searching, processing and distribution of information for the purpose of effective organization of people's activities. In other words, it is computer science. A computer consists of manycomponents, but they can generally be divided into two categories:

HARDWARE

It's a electronic componets of our PC

SOFTWARE

It's a programs in our PC. Such as programs for work with document, presentation etc. And it's a programs for right work of PC

SOFTWARE

These are programs that are installed on your computer. At same: Games, Work programs or really important programs, drivers. It help your PC work correctly.

HARDWARE

These are electronic components on your computer. At same: Motherboard, Proccessor, Graphic Card, RAM, ROM.

IT is not only code

Programmers work with not only code. They also create a design, databases and test their products.

Programmers, IDE(Integrated Development Environment)

Firstly tell about coders. For work they need a programs that help him write a code. This programs named IDE. It support so many programming language. At same: Python, Java, C, C++, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP etc.

Designers

Designers is a persons that create a different type of design. For example: web design, design of logo, game design, level design. Designer used a Figma, Blender3D, Illustrator etc. for work.

QA(Quality Assurance) Enginner

QA Enginner it is a person that doing tests of some programs, games or site. Also he find some bugs and errors.
